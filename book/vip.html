What is the VIP Service?

Welcome to Barber Crew's VIP Service, where luxury and convenience meet exceptional grooming. Designed for those who demand the best, our service offers a personalised callout experience with NoorBlendz, the barber trusted by professional footballers and athletes alike. Enjoy top-tier grooming in the comfort of your own space, tailored to your unique style and needs.

Meet NoorBlendz

As an NVQ Level 3 qualified barber with over 5 years of experience, NoorBlendz specialises in beards of all types, skin fades, tapers, and styling. Known for his precision and dedication to his craft, NoorBlendz ensures that every client leaves looking sharp and feeling their best.
