Who We Are

Welcome to Barber Crew, your trusted grooming destination in Twyford, Wargrave, and Wokingham. As a British family-run barbershop brand, we've proudly served our communities as the best local barbershops for 5+ years. Our talented team blends tradition with innovation to make you look and feel great. From Skin Fades to Luxury Hot Shaves, we're your go-to for modern grooming excellence. Serving everyone, from kids to seniors, we cater to all hair types with care and expertise.

Services

What We Do

At Barber Crew, we offer a diverse range of services tailored to meet your grooming needs. From classic cuts to luxurious pampering sessions, we have something for everyone.

Unisex Hair

Whether you're a man, woman, or child, seeking a classic or modern look, we've got you covered. Step into Barber Crew and leave feeling great with your new hairstyle.

Luxury Services

Treat yourself to a Luxury Hot Towel Steam Shave, a Cleansing Facial, or precise Eyebrow Threading. Let us pamper you and leave you feeling rejuvenated and refreshed.

Tanning

We are one of the few barbers offering high powered Hapro Sunbeds, located in our Twyford studio. Relax in a quiet part of our facility, with competitively priced sunbed treatments and aftercare.

The Team

Meet the team

Mo - Area Manager

Mo, an NVQ Level 3 qualified barber, boasts extensive experience across diverse hair types and luxury services.

As both area manager and adept barber at Barber Crew for 5+ years, he seamlessly combines grooming expertise and conversation to ensure the highest quality of service.

His wealth of experience and creativity makes him the trusted choice for so many of our customers.

Signature Service: Wahl Certified Luxury Hot Towel Shave - Experience the ultimate grooming luxury as a hot towel soothes your skin, followed by a precision steam shave for a smooth, refreshed finish.

Location: Wargrave & Wokingham.

Marius - Principal Stylist

With over 20 years of barbering and hairdressing experience, Marius is a true master of his craft, proficient in all services.

As the Principal Stylist at our Wokingham studio, Marius brings unparalleled expertise and creativity to every appointment, ensuring each client leaves feeling confident and refreshed.

His dedication to excellence and passion for his work make him a valued member of the Barber Crew team.

Signature Service: Barberlux Package - Enjoy any haircut of your choice paired with a luxurious hot towel steam shave and a rejuvenating mini facial to leave you feeling pampered and revitalized.

Location: Wokingham.

Ruby - Principal Stylist

As the Principal Stylist at our Twyford studio, with 12+ years of experience, Ruby is not just a stylist; she's a pillar of the community.

Specializing in both men's and ladies' hair services, Ruby's expertise spans a wide range of styles and preferences, a master of all.

Her dedication to her craft and her clients is evident in every appointment, ensuring each individual leaves more than satisfied.

Signature Service: Eyebrow Threading - Ruby's unparalleled skill in eyebrow threading sets her apart. Experience precision and perfection as she shapes and defines your brows with meticulous attention to detail.

Location: Twyford.

Sunny - Principal Barber

With over 30 years of experience under his belt, Sunny is not just a barber; he's a legend in the industry. As one of our most senior team members, his expertise and skill are unmatched.

Specializing in fades, beards, classic cuts, and everything in between, Sunny's mastery knows no bounds. Whether you're looking for a sharp fade, a precise beard trim, or a timeless classic cut, Sunny delivers with finesse and precision.

Signature Service: Barberlux Package - Enjoy any haircut of your choice paired with a luxurious hot towel steam shave and a rejuvenating mini facial to leave you feeling pampered and revitalized.

Location: Twyford & Wargrave.

Aimie - Barber

Starting as a trainee and originally trained in hairdressing, Aimie has flourished over her time in the Barber Crew family, honing her skills and mastering the art of barbering from classic cuts to skin fades.

Energetic, bubbly, and damn good at what she does, Aimie brings a contagious energy to every appointment. Her passion for her craft shines through in her work, ensuring each client leaves with a smile on their face and a fresh look they love.

Signature Service: Classic Scissor Cut - Whether you're after a timeless look or a modern twist, Aimie has the best scissor skills in the area and will leave you looking sharp and stylish

Location: Wokingham.

Becca - Stylist

Meet Becca, the versatile stylist at both our Twyford and Wargrave studios. With over 5 years of experience in hairdressing, Becca initially joined us as a junior barber, honing her skills in the art of unisex hair.

From skin fades to classic cuts, Becca's expertise goes beyond traditional barbering. Leading both ladies' and men's hair services, her passion and dedication to her craft shine through in every appointment.

Signature Service: Ladies Hair Wash, Cut & Finish - Relax as Becca works her magic, leaving you with a stunning hair wash, precision cut, and flawless finish that will leave you feeling confident and beautiful.

Location: Twyford & Wargrave.
