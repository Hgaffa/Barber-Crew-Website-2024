Who We Are

Welcome to Barber Crew, your trusted barbershop chain in Twyford, Wargrave, and Wokingham. As a British family-run barbershop brand, we've proudly served our communities as the best local barbershops for 5+ years. Our talented team blends tradition with innovation to make you look and feel great. From Skin Fades to Luxury Hot Shaves, we're your go-to for modern grooming excellence. Serving everyone, from kids to seniors, we cater to all hair types with care and expertise.

Services

What We Do

At Barber Crew, we offer a diverse range of services tailored to meet your grooming needs. From classic cuts to luxurious pampering sessions, we have something for everyone.

Unisex Hair

Whether you're a man, woman, or child, seeking a classic or modern look, we've got you covered. Step into Barber Crew and leave feeling great with your new hairstyle.

Luxury Services

Treat yourself to a Luxury Hot Towel Steam Shave, a Cleansing Facial, or precise Eyebrow Threading. Let us pamper you and leave you feeling rejuvenated and refreshed.

Tanning

We are one of the few barbers offering high powered Hapro Sunbeds, located in our Twyford studio. Relax in a quiet part of our facility, with competitively priced sunbed treatments and aftercare.

The Team

Meet the team

Mo - Area Manager

Area Manager and NVQ Lvl 3 Qualified Barber at our Wargrave & Wokingham studios.

With 5+ years of experience, NoorBlendz specialises in beards of all types, skin fades, tapers, and styling.

Known for his precision and expertise, Mo ensures every client leaves looking sharp and satisfied.

Signature Service: Wahl Certified Luxury Hot Towel Shave - Experience the ultimate grooming luxury as a hot towel soothes your skin, followed by a precision steam shave for a smooth, refreshed finish.

Location: Wargrave & Wokingham.

Marius - Principal Stylist

Principal Stylist at our Wokingham studio, Marius is a versatile master of all hair types and styles.

With expertise in scissor cuts, skin fades, and beard grooming, Marius delivers exceptional results that exceed client expectations.

Signature Service: Barberlux Package - Enjoy any haircut of your choice paired with a luxurious hot towel steam shave and a rejuvenating mini facial to leave you feeling pampered and revitalized.

Location: Wokingham.

Becca - Stylist

Stylist at our Twyford and Wargrave studios, Becca is the go-to for ladies' hair color and classic cuts.

Known for her expertise in creating stunning hair colors and delivering flawless scissor cuts, Becca provides personalized service that leaves clients feeling confident and refreshed.

Signature Service: Ladies Hair Colour, Wash, Cut & Finish - Relax as Becca works her magic, leaving you with stunning hair colouring, precision cut, and a flawless finish.

Location: Twyford & Wargrave.
