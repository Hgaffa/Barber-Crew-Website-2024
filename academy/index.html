The Academy

With a history of 5+ years serving Twyford, Wargrave, and Wokingham, our academy cultivates the next generation of barbers. Through hands-on mentorship and a holistic curriculum, we blend technical mastery with creative flair, preparing students to excel in the dynamic realm of modern & classical barbering. Join us to ignite your passion, elevate your skills, and embark on a fulfilling career in the ever-evolving world of barbering.

Syllabus

What We Teach

Our immersive curriculum delves deep into foundational techniques, specialized services, and the vital art of communication and business skills. With our unique Barber Crew approach, master the essence of modern barbering, paving the way to a rewarding and thriving career.

Barbering Foundations

Master the foundational techniques of barbering, including clipper and scissor work, understanding hair types, basic cutting methods, beard grooming, and adherence to health and safety standards.

Client Consultation & Management

Acquire effective client communication and consultation skills, essential product knowledge, and the art of cultivating strong client relationships. Unlock the Barber Crew process, ensuring a holistic and customer-centric experience.

Specialised Services

Dive into advanced techniques such as skin & taper fades, intricate hair designs, beard sculpting, straight razor shaves, hot towel treatments, and the provision of luxury grooming services.

Courses

Barbering Fundamentals

Ideal for beginners seeking fundamentals or experienced barbers aiming to sharpen skills, our 2-week course offers a comprehensive dive into the world of barbering. Covering:

  • Health & Safety, Tool Management, Skin Conditions.
  • Hair Types, Textures, Head Shapes, Consultation Skills.
  • Cutting Techniques, Fade Variations, Styling Methods.
  • Beard Grooming, Luxury Services, Barber Crew Standards.
  • Assessment & Practical Application.

Our curriculum blends theory with hands-on practice, providing mentorship, feedback, and personalized guidance throughout as well as assessment. Barber Crew Diploma awarded to course graduates following examination and potential placements across the Barber Crew studios.

Duration: 2 weeks. 4 days a week (Monday-Thursday 10AM to 4PM)

Qualification: Barber Crew Academy Diploma. Not NVQ accredited.

Barbering Mastery - NVQ Lvl 3

Tailored for aspiring barbers seeking mastery or professionals aiming for NVQ Level 3 accreditation, our 8-week Barbering Mastery course offers a comprehensive exploration of the craft. This course covers:

  • Health & Safety, Tool Management, Skin Conditions.
  • Comprehensive Hair Types, Textures, Head Shapes, Consultation Skills.
  • Advanced Cutting Techniques, Fade Variations, Styling Methods.
  • Advanced Beard Grooming, Luxury Services, Barber Crew Standards.
  • In-depth Assessment & Practical Application with NVQ Lvl 3 Accredited Examinations.

Our curriculum blends advanced theory with extensive hands-on practice, providing mentorship, feedback, and personalized guidance. Graduates attain mastery of all core pillars of barbering, securing an NVQ Level 3 qualification and the prestigious Barber Crew Academy Diploma. Additionally, successful graduates may explore potential placements within our esteemed Barber Crew studios upon course completion.

Duration: 8 weeks. 4 days a week (Monday-Thursday 10AM to 4PM)

Qualification: NVQ Lvl 3 Barbering Qualification & Barber Crew Academy Diploma
